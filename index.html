Inspector Mary: Learn How Others Do It

Posted on Friday, January 24, 2025

Have you ever noticed the "Inspect" link when you right click on a webpage?

I have, and when I clicked on it, a screen popped up that looked interesting. But I really didn't know until today what I was looking at or how valuable it can be.

It turns out that this "Inspect" allows you to look into the HTML for that webpage. So you can see the coding the developer used for that page. This works for informational websites like Khan Academy and some news sites. A way to learn how the big guys do it!
